intermediate push-pull-legs workout plan

this workout plan is best suitable for those who have been working out for at least 6 months.

do not start lifting heavy weights,remember,the more you feel the exercise fatigue on that particular muscle the more will you see the results.

there is no sense in lifting heavy weights or doing ego lifting because that never helps to achieve your dream goal or physique.
